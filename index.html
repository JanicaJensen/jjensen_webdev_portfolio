About



My name is Janica Jensen and I am a soon-to-be retired high school teacher of 10 years. I taught Spanish of all levels to all students of all grade-levels. I have always enjoyed computers and learning new tricks-of-the-trade. Oftentimes during the workday, I will have the thought: Wow, I wish there was an app for this. And I decided to take matters into my own hands and learn how to develop. My hope is to be able to take ideas, simplify them down into manageable chunks, and make them happen for myself and other educators. Kind of like we do for our students.
